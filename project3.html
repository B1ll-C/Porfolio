• Groot Web

    GROOT WEBSITE is a powerful, all-in-one platform designed for the ongoing monitoring, analysis, and management of tree health. By integrating data collected from the GROOT mobile application, this platform offers users a centralized, easy-to-navigate dashboard where they can assess various health metrics, detect pests and diseases, and gain critical insights into the factors impacting tree vitality. Users can track real-time health updates, visualize trends over time, and access recommendations for proactive care. With GROOT WEBSITE, forestry professionals, environmentalists, and conservationists are empowered to enhance tree preservation efforts, promote ecosystem health, and make informed decisions for sustainable management and care.
