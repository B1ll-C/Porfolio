• Groot Mobile

    GROOT is an innovative mobile application designed to help users identify indigenous tree species, detect pests and diseases, and monitor the overall health of trees. With its advanced features, GROOT enables users to easily recognize various tree species and learn about their unique characteristics. The app also provides a powerful pest and disease identification tool, allowing users to spot potential threats to tree health and take preventive measures. Additionally, GROOT offers comprehensive health monitoring capabilities, helping users track the condition of trees over time and gain valuable insights into their growth, vitality, and preservation needs. Ultimately, GROOT is an essential tool for tree care, conservation, and effective preservation, promoting healthier trees and sustainable ecosystems.
